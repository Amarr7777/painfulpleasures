Why choose us?

Experience

"At Painful Pleasure, our visionary founder and creative director, Aditya, has cultivated a wealth of artistic experience. With dedicated years to the art of tattooing, our team offers a seamless and professional tattooing experience."

Safety

Painful Pleasure prioritizes your safety by adhering to rigorous sterilization practices and using disposable equipment. We create a clean and worry-free tattooing environment to ensure your peace of mind throughout the process.

Quality

Our dedicated team of skilled artists, led by visionary founder Aditya, ensures each tattoo reflects artistic excellence, delivering a high-quality and unique masterpiece that perfectly embodies your individuality.

At Painful Pleasure, you can trust that we go above and beyond to create a safe and enjoyable tattooing experience for every client. Your well-being is paramount to us, and we take every precaution to ensure you leave our studio with both a beautiful tattoo and peace of mind.

Sanjay Amrithraj
Tattoo Artist at Painful Pleasure

As a passionate tattoo artist hailing from Imphal, Manipur, I have spent a decade refining my skills in realism, color, black work, and geometric styles. Art has always been my driving force, and through tattooing, I find joy in sharing my creations with others, enriching their lives with meaningful body art. Painful Pleasure is my dedicated creative avenue, where I embark on a lifelong journey of artistic exploration in the captivating world of tattoos.
